2.Surfing The World’s Best Waves at Coxos, in Ericeira.

Portugal undoubtedly possesses some of Europe's top surfing waves. Coxos, a right-hand barrel wave that crashes over flat rocks in Ericeira, must be considered the best wave in Portugal. One of the top surf locations in the world, Ericeira has been designated as a World Surf Reserve. Thirty excellent surf locations are within driving distance, along with nine world-class breaks.

3.Learn To Ski In The Ski Resort Serra de Estrela, Loriga.

The slopes in Portugal are unbeatable for a unique skiing experience. The ski area at Serra de Estrela only has four lifts, seven runs, and 130 metres of vertical fall, so it will never be able to compete with Whistler or other ski resorts, but it is undoubtedly a rare opportunity to ski in one of Europe's warmest nations. Although there are a few trails for intermediates and professionals as well, the resort is perfect for novices.

4.Coasteering Algarve In Sagres.

he rugged cliffs, secret beaches and stunning sea caves of the Algarve coast are made for coasteering, an adventure sport that includes jumping, swimming and climbing. The best place to try it is the Costa Vicentina Natural Park, on the coast between Lagos and Sagres in the western Algarve. Climbing orange cliffs is fun, but splashing in crystal clear waters and exploring secret caves is one of the best ways to discover Portugal's natural beauty. If you've never coasteered, it's best to hire a local guide. Because they know exactly when the tide is coming and the safest places to jump and swim.

5.Water Touch Bungee Jumping Albufeira marina in the south of Portugal.


There are only a few water bungee jumping sites in the world, and one of them is at Albufeira Marina in southern Portugal. Participants ride a crane 40 meters in the air, jump and dip their heads into the cool Atlantic waters before returning to the platform. A fun way to overcome your fear of heights. The sport was introduced to the Algarve by Carlos Mosco Dionisio, a somewhat eccentric bungee jumper born in Portugal. He holds the world's record for of the longest bungee jump.
